Blast & Build

Blast through puzzles and build your dream island paradise!

About the Game

Ohana Island: Blast & Build combines exciting match-3 puzzle gameplay with island-building adventure.

Blast through colorful puzzles to earn resources and unlock construction options to transform your island into a paradise.
Enjoy hours of relaxing gameplay, complete challenges, and explore stunning island landscapes.
Build homes, gardens, and more, while solving puzzles that bring you closer to your dream island.

How to Play

Ohana Island: Blast & Build match-3 puzzles by swapping and matching colorful blocks to create powerful blasts.

  • Earn Resources

    Complete puzzles to earn resources that help you build your island.

  • Create Your Dream Island

    Use your resources to build homes, gardens, and other island structures.

  • Unlock New Areas

    Progress through levels to unlock new areas of your island, expanding your paradise.

  • Special Boosters

    Use boosters and power-ups to solve tougher puzzles and speed up your progress.

Features

Tropical Island Adventure: Explore a stunning island and build your dream paradise while solving puzzles.

  • Challenging Puzzles: Hundreds of levels with varying difficulty to keep you engaged.

  • Match-3 Puzzle Gameplay: Blast through colorful match-3 puzzles for fun and relaxation.

  • Time-Limited Events: Participate in special events to earn extra rewards and exclusive items.

  • Island Customization: Design and build various structures like houses, gardens, and more to make your island unique.

  • Offline Mode: Enjoy the game even when you're offline, with all the gameplay available without an internet connection.
